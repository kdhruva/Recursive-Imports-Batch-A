The Project

Strong Sentiment

Investiment is a custom trained GPT-2 model that learned from around 6000 tweets from Twitter.

Interactive

Investiment will find the sentiment if a stock based off of any tweet you provide it.

Timeline

Investiment was completed by teenagers over the course of three weeks this summer!

Extensions

Some extensions to this project include adding a neutral sentiment or finding the sentiment of a stock given the ticker!
